Template Options
You can use book information in the note title and content templates. You can use a parameter name surrounded with double curly braces - for example, {{ short title }}. The preview pane will show you how your template will render with an example book. Available parameters: - full_title: the full title of the book. Note that this may include colons, which aren't legal note name in Obsidian (use short_title` instead) - short_title: the title of the book up to the first colon - publication_year: the publication year of the book's first edition
